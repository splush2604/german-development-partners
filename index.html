Who we are

Two Germans. Decades of craft. One standard.

German Development Partners was founded by two Germans who now live in Bali. One has spent years developing property across Bali; the other spent decades on German construction sites as a craftsman and quality controller.

We combine local development know-how with German engineering discipline — so the finish you'd expect in Germany meets the lifestyle and returns of Bali. We deliver projects in both Bali and Lombok.

If you want to build on Bali or Lombok yourself — from securing land and designing the villa to construction — we guide the process and own the part that matters most: quality control during the build.
